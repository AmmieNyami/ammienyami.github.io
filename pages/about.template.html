Games

My favorite games are the Touhou Project games, UNDERTALE and DELTARUNE, and Minecraft.

The first game I've really gotten into was Minecraft. I used to play a lot of modded Minecraft all day, but nowadays I don't really play it as much.

I also used to be really into UNDERTALE and DELTARUNE. I don't play those games as much these days, but they're still an inspiration for creating my own games.

I used to play osu! a lot too. In fact, before I started drawing frequently, I only used my drawing tablet to play osu! basically. Though now I don't play it as much anymore.

Nowadays, the Touhou games are by far my favorite games and my biggest inspiration for creating my own projects. I started playing them after constantly receiving recommendations for Touhou song covers from ViddyWell on YouTube, and after getting curious enough about the games and their music to research Touhou and eventually play Touhou Koumakyou ~ the Embodiment of Scarlet Devil as my first Touhou game.

My favorite Touhou games are Touhou Eiyashou ~ Imperishable Night and Touhou Kanjuden ~ Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom. I love both of these games for their stories and soundtracks, both of which are absolutely great.
